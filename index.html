ATMASIDDHI SHASTRA

Who am I? Where have I come from? What is my true purpose? Questions spark the beginning of an inner journey. Atmasiddhi Shastra is a 142-verse masterpiece, composed by 19th century self-realised saint Shrimad Rajchandraji. Quenching the genuine thirst of a seeker, Shrimadji shares six spiritual truths in this clearest, most cogent outline of the path.

World-renowned spiritual leader Pujya Gurudevshri Rakeshji reveals the hidden treasures embedded within every verse. A step-by-step guide to self-realisation through a stirring dialogue between an experienced Master and an earnest seeker. A spiritual revolution awaits all those who seek wholeheartedly within these pages.

About the Scripture

125 years ago, spiritual luminary Shrimad Rajchandraji inked an epic scripture, Atmasiddhi Shastra. Written in about 1.5 - 2 hours, yet containing the essence of all spiritual wisdom, Shrimadji's magnum opus is an encyclopaedia of spirituality and the heart of the inner path.

The Six Spiritual Truths

Since time eternity, we have pondered over the nature of our existence and our place in the universe. Atmasiddhi Shastra takes you on an unforgettable journey through six universal truths that represent our supreme reality. These six fundamentals create a crystal clear and indisputable path to eternal bliss.

1. Soul exists

2. Soul is eternal

3. Soul is the doer of karma

6. The is a path to liberation

5. There is liberation

4. Soul is the receiver of the fruits of karma
